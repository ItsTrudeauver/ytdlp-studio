Access / cookies ?

No Studio account and no password storage. Cookies are local-only.

If you clear browser cookies, login-only videos may fail until you log into that site in the browser again.

Playlist and combining ?

Use Native playlist mode only when you want yt-dlp’s playlist features directly.

Making one long video uses yt-dlp/ffmpeg. It works best when all playlist parts use compatible video/audio formats.
